Grilled Octopus with Lemon

Tender grilled octopus, marinated in lemon and herbs, served with a side of roasted vegetables.

Ingredients

Octopus, lemon, olive oil, garlic, parsley, salt, pepper, mixed vegetables.

Preparation

The octopus is marinated for 24 hours, then grilled to perfection. The vegetables are roasted with herbs and olive oil.

Allergens

Contains seafood. May contain traces of nuts and gluten.
